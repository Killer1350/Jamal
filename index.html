About Jamal's

Jamal's Suya & Shawarma is Abuja's go-to spot for bold, fire-grilled food done right. From classic shawarmas and loaded burgers to signature agege sandwiches, suya skewers, jambalaya rice, and creamy pasta — every item is built for flavour and generous portions.

Located in the heart of Wuse 2, we're open daily from 12pm to 10pm. Whether it's a quick bite or a full spread, you'll leave satisfied.

Why customers keep coming back

From the classic Chicken Shawarma to signature creations like the Large Special, the Suyazza, and the Double Decker Burger — every category on our menu gives you strong, memorable options for whatever craving hits.
